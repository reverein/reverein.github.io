How Revere Works

Tell us about your parents' needs
Tell us about your parents and their needs including any particular health conditions, preferences and desired schedule
We find the perfect caregiver(s)
We find one or more trained and vetted home caregivers that fit your parents' preferences and specific needs
They take care of all your parents' needs
From buying groceries and running errands to administering medications to taking them to the doctor, our caregivers' visits are tailored to what your parents want.
They update you on how your parents are doing
With the Revere app, you always have visibility about your parents and their health. The caregiver updates the app with notes after every visit, your parents medical documents to their blood pressure and glucose levels. Speak to them or text them anytime with any questions or directions.

Help with Anything your Parents Need

Chronic Disease Management

Our trained nurse practitioners help your parents manage their chronic diseases like diabetes and blood pressure by checking vitals regularly.

Housekeeping

Help with household needs like cleaning, dishes, laundry and trash removal.

Medication Purchase
and Reminders

Refilling prescriptions to timely reminders for taking medication and vitamins, drinking water, and other wellness prompts.

Cooking and Groceries

Purchasing appropriate groceries to cooking tailored meals depending on your parents' health requirements and taste preferences

Transportation

Helping your parents' mobility by chauffeuring to doctor's appointments, social events, and other activities.

Activities

Supporting healthy physical activities in and outside home, like walking, yoga and exercises

Regular Checkup

Any time personalized check-up service to help with anything that comes up, and ensure peace of mind.

Personal Grooming

Assisting with personal hygiene and grooming, like bathing and dressing

You are never far from your parents,
with Revere caregivers and the 
Revere app

Check in anytime

With the revere app, you can text or call your parents' revere provider anytime to check in on your parents. Your provider will send you a message before and after a visit, as well as if anything urgent comes up

Track your parents' health

Revere caregivers check your parents vitals on every visit and work with their diagnosticians to update latest reports. All this data is then uploaded to the revere app. So you can track their health from afar

Stay informed 

See which Revere provider scheduled to visit your parents and when, and what they did/will do during the visits. Let them know if you want them to something particular.

Share moments

Your revere provider shares photographs of your parents from every visit, and share your moments with them too. Doesn't matter if they can Skype or not. With revere you are always close
